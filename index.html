How To Use
Quick reference guide for the RCM Dashboard
🔐 Check In As — Daily Start of Shift
How to check in
1 Click the CHECK IN AS dropdown in the top-right header and select your name.
2 Enter your 4-digit PIN when prompted. Your PIN is private — only managers can look it up in the Staff Roster.
3 You're in. Your name appears in the header badge and you'll now receive alerts for any new assignments.
What checking in does
→ Notifications — whenever an admin assigns you a new task, a large alert appears center screen with the task details and plays a sound. It stays until you click OK. 🔴 ASAP = urgent marimba chime  ·  🟡 Next = bright ding.
→ Management staff get instant full admin access — no separate unlock needed. The header badge shows 🔓 [Name]. Everyone else sees 🔒 LOCKED and still needs the PIN unlock to edit.
💡
Sound: notification sounds play automatically all shift — even while you're working on something else in the same tab. Forgot your PIN? Ask a manager — they can view or regenerate it from your profile in the Staff Roster. Signing out: select — Sign Out — from the dropdown. Management access is revoked immediately.
⚡ Live Status Board
→ Shows who is assigned where today across all 10 offices and all RCM categories. Resets automatically at midnight Central Time.
→ Staff tokens are split tiles — colored initials block on the left, first name on the right. Active tokens on the live board have a green pulsing ring.
→ Drag any token from the roster strip or another cell into a grid cell. Each cell holds up to 4 tokens. Tokens stay on the board until removed or the board resets at midnight.
→ Right-click a token in a cell to send them to Lunch, Away/Off, or remove them from that spot.
→ Hover over any token to see a quick-view card — shows what the staff member is working on (green dot) and any newly assigned tasks (🔴 ASAP / 🟡 Next) with due dates and who assigned them.
→ Ghost text at the top of each cell shows the Assignment Board default. OWN TEAM means that office handles it in-house.
→ Changes sync automatically within seconds — every location sees the same state in real time.
📅 Insurance Verification Date
→ Each office's Ins Verification cell shows a Verified to: date. It never clears at midnight — it persists until manually updated.
→ Click the date field directly on the live board to update it. Select your name when prompted — every change is logged to the Records tab (IV Date Records) with who updated it, the old date, and the new date.
→ A color-coded dot next to "Verified to:" shows urgency at a glance:
Red flash — Not set, or not verified through tomorrow. Update immediately.
Yellow — Verified 2–3 days ahead. Plan to update soon.
Green — Verified 4+ days out. All good.
📤 Claims (Sending) — Resubmit Alerts
→ Both Claims (Sending) columns (Old PMS and Oryx) show a Resubmit: row for claims flagged Must Resubmit in the INS AR list that are approaching or past the 1-year timely filing deadline.
⚠️ PRIORITY Approaching deadline — DOS is 358–365 days old. 7 or fewer days left to resubmit.
⛔ OVERDUE Past deadline — DOS is 365+ days old (from 1/1/2025 onward). Timely filing window has closed.
→ Updated daily at 5:20 AM CT automatically. Dashboard refreshes every 15 minutes.
⏰ Aging Alerts (Timely Filing)
→ Each aging column (Old PMS and Oryx) shows alert badges at the bottom of each office cell, split by PMS type. These track claims at risk of missing the 1-year timely filing deadline.
→ Only unworked claims with no aging status set are counted. Claims already being worked (e.g., RESOLVED PER REPORT, CK IN MAIL, OM) are excluded.
→ Data refreshes daily at 7:07 AM CT automatically. The dashboard re-checks every 15 minutes.
⚠️ PRIORITY Approaching deadline — Date of Service is 358-365 days old. 7 or fewer days left to file.
⛔ OVERDUE Past deadline — Date of Service is 365+ days old (from 1/1/2025 onward). Timely filing window has closed.
🚨 Action required: If you see OVERDUE or PRIORITY badges for your office, check the Master Aging INS AR list in ClickUp immediately. These claims need urgent attention — either file, appeal, or update the aging status to clear them from the count.
📬 Posting Completion %
→ The Oryx Posting column shows a Posted: completion percentage for each office, pulled from the EOB/ERA Posting Goals list in ClickUp.
→ This tracks how much of the office's production has been posted (Collection / Production). Updated daily.
23% Red flashing — Under 50%. Critical — posting is significantly behind.
65% Yellow flashing — 50–74%. Warning — posting needs attention.
95% Green — 90%+. On track or complete.
75–89% No marker — acceptable range, keep going.
👤 Staff Profile
→ Click any staff token (on the live board, roster strip, or roster tab) to open their profile. Update name, role, department, manager, home office, phone, email, responsibilities, and vacation dates. Hit Save Profile to apply.
→ Active green pills next to the staff member's name show what they're currently working on and any Newly Assigned tasks they've checked — visible to anyone who opens the profile.
→ The Check-In PIN field (next to Initials) is only visible to management / unlocked admins. Use it to look up or regenerate a staff member's PIN if they forget it.
→ Set Vacation Start / End dates to automatically move a staff member to the Off list while they're away. They return to active on the day after their end date.
📋 Currently Working On
→ A reusable daily checklist inside each staff profile. Add tasks once — they stay saved and reset to unchecked each morning at midnight.
Unchecked In the list, not started yet for today.
● Active Currently on this — shows as a green blinking pill in the profile header and in the live board hover tooltip.
✓ Completed Done for the day — strikethrough + ↩ Reopen button. Resets at midnight.
→ Check a task to go Active → check it again to mark it Completed. Click ↩ Reopen if you marked it done by mistake. Delete permanently removes it from the list.
📌 Newly Assigned 🔒 Add / Delete — Admin Only
→ Admins can push tasks directly to a staff member from inside their profile. Each task includes a priority, an optional due date, and who assigned it. Staff are notified automatically if they're checked in.
→ To add a task: click 🔒 Unlock to Add Assignment (or check in as management), fill in the description, pick priority and assigned-by, set a due date, then click + Add.
START ASAP NOW — Start this immediately. Triggers an urgent notification with a marimba chime.
START NEXT — Start after finishing the current assignment. Triggers a bright ding notification.
→ Check the checkbox on a task to mark it in progress — the row turns green and a pill appears in the profile header and live board hover card.
→ ✓ Resolve is available to all staff — marks the task done (muted strikethrough style). If you resolve by mistake, click ↩ Reopen to bring it back. Delete is admin-only and permanently removes the task.
→ All unresolved tasks appear in the live board hover tooltip so managers can see a staff member's workload at a glance without opening the profile.
🗂 Assignment Board 🔒 Admin Only
→ The permanent reference layout — who covers what for each office and category. These names appear as ghost text on the Live Board.
→ Management staff who are checked in can edit immediately. Others click Unlock to Edit and enter the manager PIN. When unlocked, drag tokens to set assignments — Remove buttons appear on each token.
→ Use OWN TEAM to mark that an office handles a category themselves. Click ↺ Reset Defaults to restore the original layout.
👥 Staff Roster 🔒 Admin Only
→ Full staff directory — role, department, manager, home office, phone, email, responsibilities, and Check-In PIN. Management staff who are checked in can edit freely. Others click Unlock to Edit and enter the manager PIN.
→ When unlocked, the PIN column is visible in the table. Click any row to open that staff member's profile and edit details or view / regenerate their PIN.
→ To deactivate a staff member: open their profile → click Move to Inactive. They move to the Inactive department instead of being permanently deleted.
→ Management always sorts to the top of the table; Inactive staff sort to the bottom in gray.
🚨 Help Requests & Help Records
→ Click 🚨 Need Help in the header to create a help request. Drag the red token into a Live Board cell to flag which office/category needs help.
→ Click the pulsing red token to open it — add chat messages, mark helpers, and update the checklist. Click Mark Helped when someone steps in (token turns green). Click Resolve & Remove to archive it.
→ The Records tab (management only) stores all resolved help requests, IV date changes, and staff check-in/out history — each in its own sub-tab. Individual records can be deleted and logs can be cleared (admin PIN required).
☁ Saving, Syncing & Backup
→ Every change saves automatically to the cloud — no publish needed. Look for ☁ Synced in the header to confirm.
→ The dashboard checks for updates every 15 seconds — any location sees the same state automatically.
→ The ↑ Publish button (top-right, PIN required) forces an immediate sync push if needed.
→ The ↓ Backup button downloads a full JSON snapshot of the current state to your computer — useful before making big changes.
→ An automatic backup also runs every 3 hours via GitHub — snapshots are stored for 30 days and committed to the repository, so nothing is ever lost.
→ At midnight Central Time the live board resets and Currently Working On checklists go back to unchecked. Newly Assigned tasks are not cleared — they persist until resolved or deleted.
